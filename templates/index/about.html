Who we are
We are an advanced science and deep tech start-up comprising of a passionate group of experienced scientists, doctors, business professionals and young coders. We share a vision of making genomics-based services available to all by making it cheaper, better and faster. We are unlocking the power of genomics through deep technology in every sector. We come to work everyday to facilitate #GenomicsForAll and decode life.
